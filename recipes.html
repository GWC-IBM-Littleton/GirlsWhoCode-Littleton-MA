MEAT

  • Time: 5 Minutes - 5 Months
  • Serves: 1-10 People
Ingredients Quantity
Raw Meat(any type is fine) As much as you want :)
Ceremonial Dagger(optional) 1
Ocean Water(optional) 10 Liters

Most do not use any extra cooking methods for MEAT and just eat it raw after using the proper spells. However, I will show you some methods that may add extra flavour to your MEAT

Method 1: The simplest recipe for MEAT is to ferment it with in a 500 year old barrel made of the bones of various gods. The amount of time to ferment is up to you. I would recommend letting it sit for around 3-5 years.

Method 2: This is a more complicated recipe, as it requires the source meat to have been harvested on a new moon at exactly 3:19 AM. Once the meat is acquired, bury it in a nearby forest for 13 days(make sure it is deep enough so that the scavengers cannot reach it!) While waiting, soak the ceremonial dagger in ocean water(for best results, use water from the Mariana Trench, be sure to ask for permission from the local gods!) Once the meat is done fermenting, use the dagger to cut the meat up into small, bite-sized chunks.

Once everything is finished, use your favorite flavouring spells and enjoy! Method 2 goes particularly well in a salad!

Hot Dr.Pepper®

  • Time: 10 Minutes
  • Serves: 1 Person
Ingredients Quantity
Dr.Pepper® Brand Soda 12 Ounces
Lemon Slices(optional) 3 Slices

Step 1: Pour the Dr. Pepper into a medium saucepan and heat to about 180-degrees F. (The carbonation will make it look like it's boiling but you can ignore this if you use a thermometer).

Step 2: Pour the hot Dr. Pepper into a glass and drop in the lemon slices. Enjoy!

The Forbidden Sandwich

  • Time: 5 Years and 5 Minutes
  • Serves: 1 Person
Ingredients Quantity
Wonderbread® Brand Bread 2 Slices
Wunderbar® Brand Bologna 3 Slices
Adam Bros. Farming, Inc. Brand Romane Lettuce 2 Leaves
Easy Cheese™ 1 Can

Step 1: Take all of your ingredients and put them in your pantry and forget about them.

Step 2: Awake at 2 AM 5 years later hungry and sleep deprived

Step 3: Go to kitchen with the lights off and rummage through the pantry, take out ingredients without noticing what they are

Step 4: Construct sandwhich as you would with normal sandwhich ingredients(make sure you use all of the Easy Cheese™)

Step 5: Take first bite of sandwhich

Step 6: Realize it is a really bad sandwich but be too hungry and tired to care

Step 7: Finish sandwich completely and immediately regret your life choices

Step 8: Get food poisoning and die
