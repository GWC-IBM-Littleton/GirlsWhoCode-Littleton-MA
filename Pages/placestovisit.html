Places to Visit in Massachusetts

  • Fenway Park
  • Freedom Trail
  • Museum of Fine Arts

Fenway Park

Fenway Park is a large field used for watching and playing baseball. It is the home field for the Massachussetts baseball team, the Red Sox.

Freedom Trail

The Freedom Trail is a trail that shows New England during the Reveloutionary War. Thousands of people come during the Summer time to bike or walk on this trail and learn about the many aspects of America in the 1700s and there are many cemetaries too.

Museum of Fine Arts

The Museum of Fine Arts is a vast mueseum containing hundreds of artwork from many different times and places. Exbihits include artwork from Europe (such as France), Mexico, America, and even Ancient Egypt. Artwork can vary from comtemporary, to even ancient caveman drawings.
