Third Post: School Tour

07/24/2030

Hello peoples, once again.

I'm not dead, don't worry about that. I'm still a living, breathing creature.

Sadly.

Anyways, this is not the time to dwell on me being alive.

It is time to dwell on the fact that I'm going to school once again.

Ughhhhh....

My parents dragged me to a school - it's called Silver Leaf. What kind of boring school name is that? It sounds like it came out of a fairy tale.

It is a Roman Catholic school, meaning the students have to take a class of religion each year. In 9th grade, we have to learn about the Bible.

I'm not saying that it is boring, otherwise I would be offending people. Far from that actually. I'm just saying I'm not really looking forward to school. Honestly, I just want to hibernate through winter, just like bears and squirrels do.

The school isn't half-bad. It is really big, and it has two gymnasiums! The hallways are also so weird, like there is an Even Wing and an Odd Wing and a Garden Wing.

And, the staircases are so weirdly organized it's gonna take me a heck of a long time to get used to it. Honestly, how do people get used to such a school?

Then again, it isn't gonna be my only option. I'm gonna be looking at other schools too. I am going to be a computer engineer, making giant robots and stuffs like that.

Anyway, see y'all later! I'm gonna have fun trying out other schools and seeing what AP courses I'm gonna take.

Fun.

Angie out.

Fourth Post: First Day

09/02/2030

This was one of the not-so-bad first days of school.

Then again, I've never had the worst day of school of my life, like how in some books the girl/boy/whatever-gender-I-don't-even-care has the absolute worst day ever. Late to school because alarm clock didn't wake the person up, Mom or Dad also didn't wake the person up, bad hair day, bullies, and crazy stuffs like that.

Nope. Not to normal-Angela here.

The cycle is an 8-day cycle. First period one day, eighth period another day, second period another, yadda-yadda-yadda you get what I mean.

I haven't made any friends, and I'd rather not - yet - until I know if they are good for me or not.

Yes I'm a loner but what else is new?

Anyway instead of talking about some gossip on what's new on YouTube and Instagram I can get started on my homework and get my GPA up.

That's probably going to change in the coming years, but, what can you do.

All the hallways are so confusing I was almost late to every class. Note that I said almost. That's because I just followed people that said in their loud conversations that were in my next class. Thankfully they were not late or else I would have been late.

And that definitely won't look good since I'm supposed to be supersmart because I've skipped 8th grade.

There's a ton of homework that the teachers have been giving us. Some students have been whining as if it's been a long haul. It isn't that much, really. I mean, that's what you've signed up for.

Oh yeah, I forgot to write this up, but I'm too lazy to put it back.

I am in Silver Leaf - I was too lazy to go to other schools so I just came here.

Anyway, I have to go do some homeworks.

So fun, isn't it.

So not looking forward to mid-terms.
